About Us


Tradition since 2022

Trash to Toys is an initiative started by 5 highschool students for the Project Tech Confrences Hack Your Tomorrow hackathon. Our prompt was to use technology to help realize the UN’s Sustainable Development Goal number 12, to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

Trash to Toys sets out to achieve this goal by creating a platform for sharing simple and fun projects with a focus on environmentalism.


Our Inspiration

UN SDG #12

United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Our planet has provided us with an abundance of natural resources.
But we have not utilized them responsibly and currently consume far beyond what our planet can provide.
We must learn how to use and produce in sustainable ways that will reverse the harm that we have inflicted on the planet.

SDG 12 States Four Issues:

  • Halving global food waste per capita and ensuring the efficient and sustainable use of natural resources by 2030
  • Fighting pollution, reducing the overall waste generation, and improving the management of chemicals and toxic wastes
  • Supporting companies’ transition to green infrastructures and practices
  • Making sure everyone everywhere is fully informed of ways to live in harmony with nature and eventually adopts thoughtful habits
